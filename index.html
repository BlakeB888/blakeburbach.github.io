Hey There!
The name's Blake Burbach.

Since you're here, why don't I tell you a little bit about myself?

Full Stack Software Developer

Technology has always been a part of my life, and my fascination with it has inspired me to pursue a career in software development. Prime Digital Academy gave me the tools and knowledge to do just that. I now try to find ways to blend my love for artistic creation and logical thinking to build the future.

Feel free to head on over to my GitHub to see the projects I've been working on.

Musician

I found my passion for creation through music. When I was thirteen, my uncle gave me my first guitar and I drove my family insane teaching myself how to play. Since then, I've been in a number of bands, made some killer and not so killer records both as a musician and a recording engineer, and toured the country. Through it all, I've gained a deep sense of what it truly means to create art. It's both beautiful and ugly and I wouldn't know what to do without it.

Just Life Things

When I'm not in the code or making music, you can find me in the garden with my partner, Julia. She's taught me the value in the restorative nature of getting dirty with garden building and maintenance. When we're not gardening, we like to go hiking and backpacking with our Shikoku, Shoko. Other than that, I'm probably playing some video games. ¯\_(ツ)_/¯

As you can see, I'm a pretty cool guy. Want to collaborate in the future? Have a conversation over coffee? Hit me up on LinkedIn or shoot me an email.
